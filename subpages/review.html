Review & Other Resources



Are you preparing for the unit test, or just want a little refresher on all the main ideas of the course without reading through every page? You've come to the right place! On this page you'll find various review exercises that'll give you a general summary for the entire unit and allow you to prepare for any situation where you may need to recall the information!

Stephen Chakras is a firm believer in the idea that education should be fun! Activities like Kahoot, Quizlet, Blooket and Quizziz are not only fun but also aide in the learning process! If you're looking for a more relaxed way to study without having to memorize every single word on your notes paper, then these applications can certainly help with that!
